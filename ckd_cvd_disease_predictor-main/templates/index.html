How It Works

Enter patient data (e.g., age, blood pressure) for one health snapshot. The system generates 10 sequences with 57 features (9 provided, 48 padded) for prediction. Hypertension, Diabetes, CAD: 1 = Yes, 0 = No. Specific Gravity: 0=1.005, 1=1.01, 2=1.015, 3=1.02, 4=1.025.
